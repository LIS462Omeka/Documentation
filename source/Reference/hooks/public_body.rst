#################
public_body
#################

*****
Usage
*****

Allows plugins to hook in to the top of the ``<body>`` of public themes. Content added with this hook will appear at the very top of the ``<body>``, outside any other markup on the page. 

*********
Arguments
*********

:php:class:`Omeka_View` view
    The view object
